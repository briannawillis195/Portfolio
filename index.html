As the proud daughter of an incredibly resilient mother, I've made it my lifelong mission to break free from the odds stacked against me. With the unwavering support of my incredible community and its abundant resources, not only did I overcome those odds, but I flourished. After earning my Bachelor of Arts in Humanities, I channeled my passion into the realm of public health, where I dedicated myself to bridging the gap in HIV/AIDS healthcare accessibility. Along this path, I honed a diverse skill set marked by problem-solving, sharp critical thinking, and a natural ability to collaborate. My journey has been driven by a profound gratitude for the community that nurtured my growth. Now, I've embarked on an exciting adventure in the tech industry, fueled by a burning desire to showcase how technology's transformative potential can elevate lives within our community. Join me as we harness innovation to empower and make a positive impact.
